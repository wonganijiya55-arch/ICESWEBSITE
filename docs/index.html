About ICES

Building the future of civil engineering in Malawi

Our Mission

To foster innovation and excellence in civil engineering while promoting sustainable development and professional growth among our members.

Our Vision

To be the leading organization in advancing civil engineering practices and solutions that shape a better future for Malawi.

Our Objectives

To provide a platform for knowledge sharing, professional development, and implementation of innovative engineering solutions.

Our Ethics

Upholding integrity, professionalism, and environmental responsibility in all our engineering practices.

Engineering Challenges in Malawi

Identifying and addressing critical infrastructure needs

Road Infrastructure

Deteriorating road conditions and inadequate rural connectivity affecting transportation.

Water Supply

Limited access to clean water and inadequate water distribution systems in urban areas.

Urban Housing

Growing demand for affordable and sustainable housing solutions in cities.

Drainage Systems

Insufficient urban drainage infrastructure leading to flooding during rainy seasons.

Bridge Infrastructure

Need for durable bridge solutions in rural areas to improve connectivity.

Waste Management

Improving urban waste management and recycling infrastructure.
