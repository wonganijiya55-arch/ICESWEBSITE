About ICES

The Innovation in Civil Engineering Society (ICES) is a student-led community dedicated to advancing civil engineering through hands-on learning, research, and collaboration. We bring together aspiring engineers to build solutions that matter for Malawi and beyond.

Our Mission

Empower civil engineering students with practical skills, leadership, and an innovation mindset to solve real-world challenges in infrastructure, water, transport, and resilience.

Our Vision

To become Malawi’s most vibrant hub for student-driven engineering innovation—where ideas turn into sustainable projects that impact communities.

Our Values

  • Integrity and service
  • Innovation with purpose
  • Collaboration and mentorship
  • Inclusivity and respect

What We Do

Projects & Research

From material testing to sustainable housing prototypes, we design and build projects aligned with national development goals.

Community & Outreach

We organize outreach programs, school talks, and collaborative clean-up drives to foster civic responsibility and awareness.

Workshops & Talks

Technical seminars, expert guest lectures, software bootcamps, and career guidance sessions—open to all members.

Industry Linkages

We connect students with professionals and organizations for internships, mentorship, and collaborative challenges.

Our Milestones

  • 2023: Society founded with a core team of student pioneers.
  • 2024: First national student innovation showcase with industry judges.
  • 2025: Launch of ICES community outreach and mentorship program.
