Flooded Roadways in Low-Lying Suburbs

Category: Floods • Posted: 2025-10-01

Problem

Road sections at low elevations collect water during heavy rains, causing long-term damage and transport disruption.

Suggested Solution

Deploy permeable paving in critical segments, combine with smart drainage gates linked to rainfall sensors and an AI model predicting peak flows to pre-emptively open overflow routes.
